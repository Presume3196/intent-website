Meet Your Trainer

Aaron Lim is a Certified Personal Trainer, Licensed Paramedic, Performance Enhancement Specialist, Certified Nutritionist, and Certified USA Weightlifting Coach with a degree in Biological Sciences from UC Irvine.

Aaron knows that fitness can be intimidating, especially for anyone with little to no experience or mobility concerns. He strives to provide an ideal atmosphere for each and every client, so you can align your intentions with strategies for success. This includes keeping the training session fun, rotating in new workouts to keep you engaged, providing an energy that aligns with yours, thoughtfully selecting the necessary equipment, and remaining nearby to ensure safety in all movements.

Everyone has a unique background, which means there is no one-size-fits-all solution to fitness. Aaron takes time to get to know you, understand your needs and desires, and go over a plan proposal before every program begins. This helps you and Aaron get on the same page and ensures you are 100% comfortable with every aspect of the routine.

When Aaron isn't intentionally creating fitness regimens and training clients, he is likely jamming to country music; attending Dodger games; spending time with his pup, Scully; playing tennis and hockey, or exploring local cuisine.
